The Data Void

Historically, Space Situational Awareness (SSA) infrastructure was concentrated in the Northern Hemisphere (like the UK and US radars).

This left a massive blind spot. Satellites and lethal debris would disappear from radar as they crossed the equator, leaving operators guessing their trajectories.
